Maze path statistics (CPS3525 team CGI)

Upload a 2-D maze text file: first line is rows cols, then B (background) and H (path). Entrances are H on the top row; exits are H on the bottom row. The C++ CGI enumerates all simple top-to-bottom paths and optionally checks your total path count guess.
